FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions is designed for our customers. This is the place where you can ask your questions and we will give you the right answers in real time.

Those are one of the most asked one:

How do I export my contacts from Windows Live Hotmail or Outlook.com?
Sign into your Windows Live Hotmail account. Click the down-arrow at the upper-left corner of your screen Choose People. Click Manage in the menu bar. Choose Export Save the file to your desktop. The file is now ready to be imported into your Tabellarius account.
Why are the images in my email not showing up in Gmail?
Google previously did not allow images to load without permission from you. Google now acts as a proxy for your images and hosts the images as you receive them. This is to protect your computer from any malicious harm, like viruses or malware, that can be embedded in the images. If you are not seeing the images in your email, it is most likely an issue with the email itself and you'll need to contact the sender.
I want to send an email to multiple lists. Will contacts who are on 2 or more list get duplicate emails?
You can send your email campaign to multiple contact lists. You can select the contact lists in the list selection step of the email creation process. If in two groups you have a same contact he will receive the email twice if you don’t unselect them.
How do I reuse an Email campaign that I‘ve already sent?
At some point you may want to reuse an email campaign that you have created. To reuse an email campaign all you have to do is to press the forward button and add the new recipients.

How to create a post or email on Tabellarius platform?
Go to the Template editor and start to create your unique template. Its easy you just drag and drop. After that you can choose where you want to put the text, the images, social links…. When you are done with creating, just press Save Template, choose people from the contacts and send.
How can I create new contact?
Its very easy. Click on the Contacts and after that on your right side you have an option +New Contact. Click that button and enter the name after than choose if is a person, of a company or an organization and press create. Fill the blank fields. Go to save and you are done.
How to create new message on the Tabellarius platform?
Click on the Messages and in the right corner go to +New Message. A new window will open where you write da name of the message. After that insert the template that you made. You have an option to choose email address and phone numbers to send the message. Make the decision and press send.
